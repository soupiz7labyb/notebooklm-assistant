Privacy Policy for NotebookLM Assistant

Last Updated:

This Privacy Policy describes how NotebookLM Assistant ("we", "our", or "the Extension") handles information when you use our Chrome extension.

1. Data Collection

1.1 Personal Information

NotebookLM Assistant does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. We do not have any backend servers or databases.

1.2 Local Data Storage

The Extension may store the following data locally in your browser:

  • Authentication tokens: OAuth tokens provided by Google for authentication with NotebookLM services
  • User preferences: Selected notebook ID, queue items, and other user interface preferences
  • Extension state: Information about the current state of the extension (authentication status, etc.)

All this data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API and is never transmitted to third-party servers.

2. Data Usage

2.1 How We Use Data

The Extension uses locally stored data only for:

  • Maintaining your authentication session with Google services
  • Remembering your selected notebook and preferences
  • Managing the upload queue for content you choose to send to NotebookLM

2.2 Content You Send

When you use the Extension to send content (web pages, videos, files, text) to NotebookLM:

  • The content is sent directly from your browser to Google NotebookLM services
  • We do not intercept, store, or process this content on our servers
  • The content transmission is handled entirely by Google's services

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google Services

The Extension integrates with the following Google services:

  • Google OAuth: For authentication. We use Google's OAuth 2.0 service to authenticate you with NotebookLM. This process is handled entirely by Google, and we do not receive or store your Google credentials.
  • Google NotebookLM: For content storage. When you send content through the Extension, it is transmitted directly to Google NotebookLM services. The content is subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
  • YouTube: For extracting video information. When you provide YouTube URLs, the Extension may access YouTube's public API to retrieve video metadata. This is done directly from your browser.

3.2 No Other Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any other third-party services, analytics providers, or advertising networks.

4. Permissions

The Extension requests the following permissions and explains why:

  • identity: Required for Google OAuth authentication
  • sidePanel: To display the extension interface in Chrome's side panel
  • storage: To save your preferences and authentication state locally
  • activeTab: To access the current web page when you choose to send it to NotebookLM
  • contextMenus: To add "Send to NotebookLM" option to the right-click menu
  • tabs: To interact with browser tabs when sending content
  • cookies: To access NotebookLM authentication cookies (required for API access)
  • scripting: To extract content from web pages

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally in your browser:

  • Your data is protected by Chrome's built-in security mechanisms
  • We do not have access to your locally stored data
  • Data is not transmitted over the internet except when you explicitly choose to send content to NotebookLM

6. Children's Privacy

NotebookLM Assistant is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Posting a notice in the Chrome Web Store listing if significant changes are made

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally in your browser, you have full control:

  • You can view and delete locally stored data through Chrome's extension management page
  • You can uninstall the Extension at any time, which will remove all locally stored data
  • You can revoke OAuth permissions through your Google Account settings

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Through the Chrome Web Store support page
  • Via GitHub Issues (if you have access to the repository)

This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date listed above and applies to all users of NotebookLM Assistant.
